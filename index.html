About Darshan

Magic runs in the family.

All ages

Shows designed to entertain children, families, and adults alike.

For Darshan, magic is in the blood. His father, Prof. Sathyamurthy, is a renowned magician in India who has performed thousands of shows and received hundreds of awards. As a child, Darshan often accompanied his father as an assistant and developed a deep love for the art of magic.

Darshan began performing solo at the age of 20 and has now spent the past 15 years entertaining audiences with unforgettable shows. He has delivered around 950 performances and been recognized with awards from multiple organizations in India.

Alongside his work as a full-time IT consultant in Sydney, Darshan devotes his free time to magic, creating engaging performances for people of all age groups.
