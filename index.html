About Darshan

A modern magician for Sydney events that deserve a polished guest experience.

Darshan's approach to live entertainment is confident, refined, and audience-aware. The performance style is built around elegant presentation, natural interaction, and moments of surprise that feel premium rather than overplayed.

From cocktail receptions and weddings to corporate occasions and private functions, the experience is shaped to suit the atmosphere of the event while keeping guests engaged, relaxed, and genuinely impressed.

Seeing is believing, yet it feels unbelievable when Magician Darshan steps onto the stage. His magic is designed to be a genuine feast for the eyes, built on the art of completely mesmerizing an audience.

For Darshan, magic is in the blood. His father, Prof. Sathyamurthy, is a famous magician in India who has performed thousands of shows and received hundreds of awards. As a child, Darshan accompanied his father as an assistant and absorbed the craft quickly, before beginning to perform solo from the age of 20.

Over the past 15 years, Darshan has delivered around 950 performances and received awards from many organizations in India. Alongside working as a full-time IT consultant in Sydney for the last 10 years, he has continued dedicating his free time to the art of magic and to entertaining audiences of all age groups.
